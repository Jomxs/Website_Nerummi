MODEL ART

REQUIREMENTS

  • This service is for model art only
  • Reference image files of the model are required
  • No reference sheet available? That is okay but expect trial and error with design direction, resulting in extra revisions (+100 EUR)

WHAT IS INCLUDED

  • Full body model art
  • Ready-to-rig organized & renamed PSD with detailed cutting
  • 3 free expressions upset, blush, star eyes
  • Reference sheet matching the samples
  • Head guidelines & angles for the rigger

Rigging is not included. You receive 3 free revisions. Major design changes before final delivery (such as redesigning the character) may incur an additional fee at my discretion.
